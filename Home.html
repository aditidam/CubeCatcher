Cube Catcher!

Have fun while stimulating your brain and becoming a math genius!

Description

Bored? Having nothing to do to pass time? Well you should stimulate your brain by playing Cube Catcher! Catch cubes that are either even or odd specified in the beginning of each level. This app tests and improves your quick math skills. In no time you will become a math genius!
